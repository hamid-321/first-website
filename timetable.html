The Timetable

Timetable Structure

The timetable is split into 5 days, each day consists of eight 50 minute blocks starting from 9:00AM and ending at 4:50PM. Not all of the blocks have a lesson scheduled in them, this creates free periods where a student can use the time as they please, whether for study or something else.

There are 5 groups in the first year, therefore there are 5 timetables. As I am in group 5, the timetable seen below will only correspond to members of group 5. The rest of the timetables and even lecturers timetables can be accessed from a different website made by The University Of Salford called First Year Matters, this website has timetables for every group of computer science in first year and even for the other years.
